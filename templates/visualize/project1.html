Dengue Data in the Philippines from 2016-2021

The Dengue dataset spanning from 2016 to 2021 provides a comprehensive overview of Dengue fever cases in the Philippines, according to the Department of Health's Epidemiology Bureau. The dataset includes crucial information such as location, reported cases, deaths, date of occurrence, and regional classifications. Our dashboard aims to identify the number of dengue cases and total number of deaths in each province from year 2016 to 2021.
